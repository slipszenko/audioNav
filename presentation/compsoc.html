webkitSpeechRecognition

What it is

The HTML5 webkitSpeechRecognition API, allows you to build entirely voice driven web applications. This opens up and entirely new set of possibilities within the browser, such as:

  • Voice driven games
  • New means of accessibility
  • Siri-like web application
  • Hands free mobile sites
  • Potentially faster navigation
  • Voice navigation

Possible Users

Possible users for voice navigation:

  • The disabled
  • Tech illiterate users, such as the elderly or very young children
  • Someone who is multitasking, e.g. a researcher performing an experiment, or just someone doing the dishes who wants to read at the same time.
  • ... or just someone too lazy to move the mouse.
